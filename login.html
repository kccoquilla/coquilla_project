Clean the environment and preserve the Earth for more years to come.

Saving our planet's condition from deteriorating is our ultimate goal. One of these factors that contribute to its worsened state is the excessive use of plastics. As of March 21, 2021, about 2.7 million tons of plastic waste is being produced by the Philippines (World Bank, 2021). These plastics can be very destructive to the world of nature as it doesn't decompose and may even end up in animals' ecosystems. To prevent any more damage, we must retaliate now. Are you willing to take the challenge?
